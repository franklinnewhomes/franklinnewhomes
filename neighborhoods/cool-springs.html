New Homes in Cool Springs

Modern living with unbeatable access to shopping, dining, and major employers.

Cool Springs is one of the most desirable areas in Franklin for buyers who want convenience, upscale amenities, and new construction homes. Located just minutes from I-65, Cool Springs offers easy access to Nashville while maintaining a polished suburban feel.

New homes in Cool Springs range from stylish townhomes to luxury single-family residences built by some of Middle Tennessee’s most respected builders. Many communities feature walkable layouts, community pools, greenways, and modern architectural designs.
