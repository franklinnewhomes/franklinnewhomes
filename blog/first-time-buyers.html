Top 5 Tips for First-Time Home Buyers in Franklin

Buying your first home doesn’t have to be overwhelming.

Buying your first home is an exciting milestone — especially in a market like Franklin, Tennessee. With strong schools, growing neighborhoods, and excellent new construction options, Franklin is an ideal place to start homeownership.

1. Get Pre-Approved Before You Shop

Pre-approval helps you understand your budget and shows builders that you are a serious buyer. It also prevents disappointment by keeping your search realistic.

2. Understand the New Construction Timeline

Unlike existing homes, new construction can take several months. Knowing this upfront allows you to plan your move and finances properly.

3. Choose a Reputable Builder

Not all builders are the same. Look for builders with strong reputations, quality craftsmanship, and transparent communication throughout the process.

4. Budget for More Than the Base Price

Upgrades, closing costs, and design selections can add up. Make sure you understand what’s included and what’s optional before signing a contract.

5. Work With Experts Who Know Franklin

Local experts understand Franklin’s neighborhoods, pricing trends, and builder incentives — helping you make confident decisions.
